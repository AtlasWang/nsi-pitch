08 — Use Case

Use case: contested and precise logistics

“Logistics wins wars, but current solutions fail under uncertainty.”

Neural AI has no guarantees → not trustworthy for precise logistics
Symbolic AI assumes perfect knowledge → poor at handling uncertainty and contested logistics
Days to compute optimized plans → precludes rapid replanning
Verification-centric, neurosymbolic AI will resolve the limitations of the SOTA AI and enable a revolution in logistics.
